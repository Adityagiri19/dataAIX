Project-Based Learning Roadmap

A clear path from learning to career excellence through hands-on projects.

Level 1

Foundational Project

Goal / Focus: Understand basics, tools, and setup

What Students Do: Learn domain tools, follow a guided mini project with explanations

Outcome / What They Gain: Setup confidence, basic output, tool familiarity

Level 2

Intermediate Project

Goal / Focus: Apply core concepts into a semi-real build

What Students Do: Use logic, APIs, visualizations, UI/UX, etc. to build a more functional project

Outcome / What They Gain: Independent building skills, deeper problem solving

Level 3

Capstone Project

Goal / Focus: Use all skills to solve a real-world problem

What Students Do: Plan, build, and present a full-fledged solution like a real industry task

Outcome / What They Gain: Portfolio-ready project, end-to-end understanding

Frequently Asked Questions

Got doubts? Here’s everything you need to know before starting your journey.

Who is this platform designed for?

dataAXI Lab is for students, freshers, and working professionals who want to build real-world projects and boost their resumes with practical, job-ready skills. No prior experience? No problem — our task-based modules will guide you step by step.

What do I get after completing the final project?

Once you complete the capstone project, you'll receive a verified certificate, GitHub-ready project files, and the confidence to showcase your work in interviews and on LinkedIn.

Is there any placement or interview support?

Yes! We provide project-based interview prep notes, mock interviews, and mentor support. If you perform well, we also forward your resume to hiring partners in our network.

What is the learning format?

We offer 100% self-paced, online task-based learning. You can start anytime, build at your own speed, and submit your project when ready. Mentor support is available throughout.

How much does it cost to unlock a domain?

Each domain can be unlocked with a one-time payment starting at just ₹299. This gives you lifetime access to all 3 levels of tasks — Foundation, Intermediate, and Capstone — along with certification.
